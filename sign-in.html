About the permissions required
Email read access
Deseat.me works by finding emails confirming your signup from different services providers. Your emails will not leave your browser and are not uploaded to our servers.
Email sending
Deseat.me allows you to send a GDPR removal request directly to service providers via email. An email will only be sent after showing you a preview of the entire email, and you clicking "Send".
